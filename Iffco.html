IFFCO

Empowering Indian Agriculture with Trusted Fertilizers and Crop Solutions

IFFCO (Indian Farmers Fertiliser Cooperative Limited) is one of India's largest cooperative societies, providing high-quality fertilizers and agri-products to millions of farmers. Known for trust and innovation, IFFCO plays a crucial role in driving sustainable agriculture in India.

Farmer-Centric

IFFCO supports millions of farmers with cost-effective and high-performance fertilizers.

Made in India

Proudly manufacturing world-class fertilizers and products within India for Indian agriculture.

Sustainable Growth

Committed to environment-friendly products that enhance soil health and crop yield.
